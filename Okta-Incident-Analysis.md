# Okta Incident Analysis (2023)

This project analyzes the Okta Support Case Management System data breach.

## Overview
The incident involved unauthorized access to Okta’s support system through compromised service account credentials. Attackers were able to access sensitive customer data and session tokens.

## Key Findings
- Weak credential protection allowed unauthorized access
- Lack of strong authentication controls increased risk
- Session tokens were exposed, enabling session hijacking

## Impact
- Confidential customer data exposure
- Increased risk of phishing and account takeover
- Operational and reputational damage

## Mitigation Strategies
- Enforce multi-factor authentication (MFA)
- Secure service account credential storage
- Implement strict access controls
- Monitor and log suspicious activities
